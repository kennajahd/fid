Tapenade (Olive Spread)

A delicious olive spread perfect for appetizers.

Ingredients

  • Olives
  • Garlic
  • Capers
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Combine ingredients
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Serve with bread
