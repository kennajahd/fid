Tapenade (Olive Spread)

This is a simple and delicious tapenade recipe that is perfect as an appetizer or spread. Serve it with bread, crackers, or fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pitted black olives
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon capers

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a food processor.
  2. Pulse until the mixture is finely chopped.
  3. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
  4. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later use.

Tip: Tapenade tastes even better after sitting for a few hours so the flavors can blend.
